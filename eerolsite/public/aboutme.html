About me

My name is Eero Lehtinen. I'm an 18 year old Finnish man passionately interested in creating software. As a kid I was baffled about how computers could know where the mouse is or how to display a window only using electric signals. I was determined to find out the inner workings of computation. Back then I wasn't smart enough to find out anything, though.

However, in 7th grade I was introduced to Scratch in a programming class. I created my first simple games and was instantly hooked. After that, I have been studying computer programming on my own without sacrificing on my academic performance.

My programming studies haven't gone to waste: I have made multiple intriguing personal projects on my free time. They are very diverse ranging from simple browser extensions running on javascript to complex C++ multiplayer games requiring intricate networking.

I would say that perseverance and refined analytical thinking are my best features. In my opinion, programming is all about failing multiple times before finally getting it right.
