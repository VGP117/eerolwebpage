About me

My name is Eero Lehtinen. I'm an 18 year old Finnish man passionately interested in creating software. I have always been baffled about how electric signals in computers could produce such impressive results. I was determined to find out how they worked. Back then I wasn't smart enough to find out anything.

However, in 7th grade I was introduced to Scratch in a programming class. I created my first simple games and was instantly hooked. After that, I have been studying computer programming on my own without sacrificing on my academic performance.

I graduated Three years of optional education
starting after nine years of
compulsory education in Finland"lukio" in 2018 with excellent results from the final tests. I got an Laudatur: top 5% of all candidatesL in math, English and chemistry. Also, I got an Eximia cum laude approbatur: top 15% of all candidatesE in Finnish and physics. I was quickly selected to the Tampere University of Technology to study information technology. I didn't even have to go to the entrance examination thanks to my great final exam scores. I would say that perseverance and refined analytical thinking are my best features. They have brought me this far in life.

My programming studies haven't gone to waste: I have made multiple intriguing personal projects on my free time. They are very diverse ranging from simple browser extensions running on javascript to complex C++ multiplayer games requiring intricate networking.

I'm currently serving in The Finnish Defence Forces. I'm planning to stay there for about 9 months, after wich I can continue my studies in university.
